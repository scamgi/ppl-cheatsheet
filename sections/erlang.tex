\subsection{Concurrency and Actors}
Erlang's primary strength is its support for concurrency, using the actor model.
\begin{erlangcode}
-module(hello).
-export([start/0, say_something/0]).

say_something() ->
    io:format("Hello, world!~n").

start() ->
    spawn(hello, say_something, []).
\end{erlangcode}
